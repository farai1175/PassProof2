Grade 12 Mathematics

Grade 12 Mathematics is a course that covers the following topics:

Term 1

Topics

  • Number patterns, sequences and series
  • Functions
  • Exponential and logarithmic functions
  • Trigonometry

Term 2

Topics

  • Eclidean geometry
  • Analytical Geometry
  • Differential Calculus Including Polynomials

Term 3

Topics

  • Finance, growth, decay and Annuities
  • Statistics
  • Probability and Counting principle
